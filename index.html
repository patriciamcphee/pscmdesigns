About

PSCM Designs

Hi! I'm Patti Short, in early 2019, I knew it was time to start doing more creative. I wanted to use my knowledge, skills, and passion for presenting information that grabs the reader's attention because I LOVE page layout and design!

Although I haven't had the title of Graphic Designer in over 15 years, I have used this my experience and knowledge of page layout and design to strategically design user guides and online help in such a way that helps the user rather than hinders them. Today, I bring this passion as well as instructional design (eLearning) and voice over to my list of marketable skills.

I am passionate about creating content experiences that help people achieve more with technology.
